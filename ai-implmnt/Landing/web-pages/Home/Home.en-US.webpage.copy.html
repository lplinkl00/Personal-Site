Common questions

Things people usually
want to know

Is this relevant for a small business like mine?

Yes — and especially so. Large companies have entire IT teams figuring this out. You don't. That's exactly why practical, focused guidance matters more for SMEs than anyone else.

I don't have a technical background.

Neither do most of my clients. Everything I do is explained in plain language. If you can use email and a smartphone, you can use AI — you just need someone to show you where to start.

We've tried AI tools before and got nothing from them.

That's the most common thing I hear. The tools aren't the problem — it's knowing which ones to use, for what tasks, in what way. That's what the Starting Point session is designed to fix.

How do I know this will be worth it?

The discovery call is free. If after talking to me you don't think it's worth proceeding, don't. I'd rather lose a sale than have a client who doesn't get value.

What about the EU AI Act?

For most SMEs using standard tools, the immediate impact is limited. I'll always flag what's relevant to your sector. If you're in a regulated industry, it's worth understanding — and I'll help you do that.

Will we become dependent on you?

The opposite is the goal. Every engagement is designed so your team can continue independently afterwards. I measure success by whether you still need me — and I aim for the answer to be no.
