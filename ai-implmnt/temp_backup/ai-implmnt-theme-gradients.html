The three-stop pastel gradient creates warmth in the hero without competing with the orange CTA. The blush hints at the Signal brand colour; the mint trails out to Jade.

Dark section — Deep Night background

Deep Night blends Ink to a jade-tinted dark, creating depth without pure black. The jade tint in the shadows subtly reinforces the brand without being obvious.

Cards — Blush to Mint icon · Pastel background

Alternating blush and mint card backgrounds signal which colour each service relates to. Keeps the layout varied without adding new colours.
